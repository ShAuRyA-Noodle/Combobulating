# Phase 3 Post-Event Follow-Up Cadence

Galaxy Brain — Aura — Samsung EnnovateX 2026.

The 48 hours after the Bangalore finals are the highest-leverage
window for follow-ups. Memory of the pitch is fresh in every judge's
head. Every contact made on stage day decays sharply after 72 hours.

This file scripts the follow-up cadence so neither founder freezes
on day-after exhaustion.

---

## T+0 (event evening, in Bangalore)

- [ ] Both founders sit down at the hostel within 4 h of finishing.
- [ ] Open the small notebook used during the event.
- [ ] Transcribe every judge ask, every name written down, every
  business card or LinkedIn handle, into a single `event_log.md`
  in this directory.
- [ ] Note the **single-sentence summary** of each judge's reaction:
  positive / neutral / negative + one-line context.
- [ ] Photo of the team with the deck on the projector (if not done
  on stage). Save to `aura/_trust/event_photos/` (Phase 3-only
  subfolder).

---

## T+12 h (next morning)

- [ ] Drink water. Eat something with protein.
- [ ] Re-read the event log. Fix anything ambiguous while it is
  still fresh.
- [ ] Draft the thank-you email to each judge — one per judge, not
  a templated blast. Each email:
  - Opens with a sentence that proves attention ("Thank you for the
    question about [specific thing] during our pitch.")
  - Names the specific topic the judge cared about.
  - Includes one link: GitHub repo or specific evidence file.
  - Closes with "We are happy to walk through the threat model
    audit / KPI raw data / Galaxy port details if that would be
    useful."
  - Length: 4–6 sentences. Not more.
- [ ] Get Shaurya to write, Shorya to review for tone, send by 14:00
  local time.

---

## T+24 h

- [ ] LinkedIn DM to each judge whose handle was captured. Format:
  - "Hi [name], thank you for the [exact question] feedback at
    EnnovateX yesterday. We've published [specific link]. Would
    love to stay in touch."
  - Connection request optional.
- [ ] LinkedIn post by each founder — one post each, not a duplicate.
  Use the template at `aura/_trust/social/linkedin_post.md` if
  available; otherwise a 4–6 line reflection on a specific moment
  from stage day.

---

## T+36 h (still in Bangalore, ideally)

- [ ] Tweet thread by Shaurya — use the template at
  `aura/_trust/social/x_thread_shortlist.md` with stage-day photos
  inserted. 5–7 tweets max.
- [ ] Pin the thread on Galaxy Brain's X / Twitter handle (if one
  exists; otherwise on Shaurya's personal handle with `@auragb`
  bio mention).

---

## T+48 h

- [ ] GitHub repo "star ask" — a single, low-pressure DM to anyone
  who saw the demo and seemed interested:
  > "If you found this interesting, a GitHub star at
  > github.com/ShAuRyA-Noodle/Combobulating helps the project
  > stay alive. Thanks for stopping by."
- [ ] Slack / Discord post in any community the team is part of
  (Thapar Tech, Bangalore tech meetups), linking the repo and the
  Phase 3 deck PDF.
- [ ] One post-event Twitter thread reflection — what worked, what
  did not, what we would change, with a final line on what's next
  for Aura (without committing to anything).

---

## Within T+1 week

- [ ] Email Samsung Catalyst / Samsung Ventures India contacts (if
  any judge offered an intro) with a 1-page Aura deck PDF attached.
- [ ] Draft a Phase 3 retrospective note — what we did, what
  surprised us, what's next for Aura. Commit at
  `docs/release_notes/v1.0.0-phase3-retrospective.md`. This is the
  honest post-mortem for the team, not a marketing piece.

---

## Within T+2 weeks

- [ ] Review judge feedback themes. Pick the top 2 to fold into the
  Aura roadmap. Open GitHub issues for each.
- [ ] Decide as a team: do we keep building Aura, do we open-source
  the orchestrator + trace per `plan.md` §34.3, or do we paddle to
  the next thing.
- [ ] Either way, send a final "thank you again" note to anyone who
  followed up beyond polite.

---

## What to never do post-event

- Do not chase a no. If a judge does not reply within 5 days, drop
  it. Begging signals weakness.
- Do not mass-DM judges — each note is bespoke.
- Do not post fabricated metrics from the pitch on social media.
  Stay anchored to measured numbers.
- Do not claim Samsung partnership unless Samsung writes a partner
  agreement first.

## 1. Route — optimal cheapest path

| Leg | Mode | Operator | Class | Estimated cost (₹) per person | Travel time |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Patiala → New Delhi | Train | Indian Railway | Sleeper / 3AC | 250 – 600 | ~4–5 h |
| New Delhi → Bangalore (Yeshwantpur / KSR) | Train | Indian Railway | Sleeper (SL) | ~2,000 – 2,500 | ~36–40 h |
| Bangalore station → Taj Yeshwanthpur | Auto / BMTC bus / Namma Metro Green Line + walk | local | local | 30 – 200 | 20–60 min |

Total per leg per person: **≈ ₹2,500**, two-way ≈ **₹5,000**.

PNR booking via Indian Railway IRCTC (`https://www.irctc.co.in`).
Tatkal opens ~1 day before departure for non-tatkal classes; book at
T-30 days for cheapest sleeper allotment.

Train name suggestions (verify schedule when shortlist arrives):

| Train | Route | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 12649 / 12650 Karnataka Sampark Kranti Express | Hazrat Nizamuddin → Bangalore (KJM) | Tri-weekly, sleeper available |
| 16687 Navyug Express | Jammu Tawi → Mangalore via Bangalore | Sleeper available, longer but cheaper |
| 22692 Rajdhani | New Delhi → Bangalore | Faster (~32 h) but ~3× cost — avoid unless schedule forces |

If train tickets are unavailable: SpiceJet / IndiGo Delhi → Bangalore
spot fares typically ₹4,500 – ₹7,500 in season; flag as exception
spend, push the trip total north of ₹10,000 per person.
